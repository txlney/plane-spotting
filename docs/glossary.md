# Plane Spotting and Aviation Glossary

| Term | Expansion | Description |
| ---- | ---- | ---- |
| ADS-B | **Automatic Dependent Surveillance Broadcast** | A technology that allows aircraft to transmit their position, altitude, and other critical data automatically via satellite or other sensors. |
| MLAT | **Multilateration** | A technology that uses many mathematical calculations to derive the positions of aircraft not yet transmitting ADS-B data. |


a technology that allows an aircraft to transmit its position, altitude, and other information, like velocity, automatically via satellite navigation or other sensors. This data is broadcast to air traffic control, other aircraft, and even hobbyists, who can receive and display it to track aircraft in real-time.
